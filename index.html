About Me

I believe in the power of data analytics to be an informative tool for interpreting the world around us. When analyzed effectively, data can be used to drive strategic decisions and help people make smarter decisions. In my undergrad, I studied world economics at Shanghai University of Finance and Economics. I also gained invaluable experience in big data consulting during my internship with PwC. These experiences lead me to pursue my Master of Science in Business Analytics at UC Davis. During my time at UC Davis I assisted data scientists at tech startup Engage3 with data analysis work as part of the UC Davis practicum project. My educational background, experience, and passion allow me to bring unique value to the table.

In my spare time, I enjoy playing musical instruments (accordion and ukulele), learning new languages, reading detective novels and sci-fi books, playing volleyball, hiking and travelling. I also enjoy bringing laughter and joy to people around me.
